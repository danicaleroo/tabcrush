Privacy Policy

tabcrush does not collect, store or share personal information in any way. No page under the domain https://www.tabcrush.com collects, stores or shares personal information in any way.

Tabcrush Google Chrome Extension does not collect, store or share personal information in any way. The extension requests the browser's tabs permission solely to execute chrome.tabs.getCurrent and chrome.tabs.update, redirecting the new tab to https://www.tabcrush.com/new.

All tasks and settings are saved locally in your browser. No analytics, tracking scripts, remote code, or network requests to third-party services are used.

All code is open source and available on GitHub.

Questions? Open an issue.
